How do I delete a scene?

No, you can't delete a scene. You can only clear it.

How to rename project name?

Click on your project, then click on the edit icon to rename the project.

How to zoom?

Switch to zoom mode by clicking on the zoom button. Then, you can zoom with two fingers or move the canvas with one finger.

How to change color?

Click on the color button, then choose the color you want.

How to import an image, export scene, and export the whole project?

To import an image from the gallery, click on the 'more' button, then click on the 'Import Image' button. To export the current scene, click on the 'Export Scene as PNG' button. To export the whole project, click on the "Export Whole Project" button.

How do I copy paste a scene?

Long press on the scene preview, a popup will show up with Copy and Paste buttons. Click "Copy" and go to the scene where you want to paste, then long press on the preview and click the "Paste" button to paste.
